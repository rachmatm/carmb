Car Background Replacer

Upload a car photo to remove the background and replace the license plate.
The AI model (~44 MB) downloads once per browser and runs entirely on your device; no server, no uploads.
Speed depends on your hardware and browser resources (e.g. low battery may slow things down).
This is a demo; production will use a dedicated server and a superior AI Model.
